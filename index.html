FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

What kinds of payments do you accept?

Payment is expected at the time of service; we offer several payment options:

Cash or Personal Check

Credit Cards: We accept Master Card, Visa, Discover and American Express.

We also offer alternate financing options: Scratchpay, CareCredit, LendingClub, and GreenSky.

Do you still accept new patients?

Yes! We're happily accepting new patients and would love to welcome you to Sherman Oaks Cosmetic & Implant Dental Group. Call (818) 990-4121 to book your first visit.

What if I have an after-hours emergency?

If you have a dental emergency outside our normal business hours, please call our office at (818) 990-4121. Our voicemail will provide instructions on how to reach the doctor on call.

What if I need to see a specialist?

We have an in-house team of specialists including periodontists, orthodontists, and implant dentists. For care outside our scope, we partner with a trusted network of referral specialists.

How do you deal with dental phobics?

We understand dental anxiety is very real. Our team is trained in gentle techniques, and we offer sedation options including nitrous oxide for patients who need extra comfort during treatment.

What types of insurance do you accept?

Insurance can be complicated. Call our office and we'll quickly let you know whether we work with your specific plan and what your benefits look like — before you commit to anything.
